Justin Tam is a musician, writer + programmer born in Sydney. He works between the disciplines of fiction, composition + sound design, and game + narrative design.

He co-founded the music/art collective Eternal Dragonz .

Most recently, he was online content developer + communications specialist at the Sydney Opera House .

He makes music under the name Tzekin and has performed in Sydney, Melbourne, Tokyo, Seoul, Hong Kong, Shenzhen, London, LA, NYC, and SF. He has produced for vocalists (Sam Rui, Alice Vicious, MOLDY, Slodown) and plays the saxophone.
