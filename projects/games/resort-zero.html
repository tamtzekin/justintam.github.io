Resort Zero

As part of the Haunted PS1 gamejam, we had a month to make a PSX-style horror game to the theme of 'architectural horror'.

Hotel Zero is a first-person narrative-heavy horror game. You play a travel writer interviewing residents of a hotel via their room intercoms. Each resident's ideologies are tied to the hotel's very nature in increasingly disturbing ways.

I was lead writer on the project, composed the music and sound design, and also worked on narrative scripting and level design.

Built in Unity, scripted in Ink + Inky editor, project management using Trello.
